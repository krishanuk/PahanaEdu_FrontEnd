Help & User Guide

Dashboard Book Catalog Item Management Help

Getting Started

  1. Login with your username & password.
  2. Admins: use Item Management to add/update/delete books.
  3. Cashiers: open Book Catalog, add to cart, and checkout.
  4. Use Customer screens to add or edit customer records.
  5. To leave the system, click Logout (see Exit below).

FAQs

I can’t see “Item Management”.
This feature is for Admin only. Ask an admin to grant access.
How do I add items to a bill?
Go to Book Catalog, choose quantity and click Add to Cart. Open the cart to checkout.
How do I print a bill?
After checkout or bill creation, click Print on the bill screen to generate a PDF/printable page.
Login failed.
Check your username/password. If locked, wait 15 minutes or contact an admin.

Keyboard & UI Tips

  • Tab to move between fields, Enter to submit forms.
  • Inline validation highlights fields with issues.
  • Use the search box on catalog or item list to filter quickly.
